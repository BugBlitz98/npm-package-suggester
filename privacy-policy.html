Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2025

NPM Package Suggester is a Chrome Extension designed to enhance your browsing experience on npmjs.com by suggesting alternative npm packages.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. All operations are performed locally within your browser.

Use of Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API solely to save user preferences and improve user experience. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. The extension operates independently within your browser environment.

Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, security risks are minimal. We are committed to maintaining a secure and private user experience.
